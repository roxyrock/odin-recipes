A delicious spaghetti recipe for an unforgettable culinary experience

Pasta, garlic, oil, grated cheese and lot of fun!

Ingredients:

  • 160 g spaghetti
  • 2-3 spoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic lends
  • 2 spoons grated cheese
  • Pepper, salt

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta for the amount of time indicated on the package.
  2. Toast the garlic with olive oil and add a little chilli.
  3. Mix the pasta with the freshly made garlic seasoning.
  4. Add the grated cheese on top and serve hot.
