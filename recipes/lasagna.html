A delicious lasagna recipe for an unforgettable culinary experience

Ragu, bechamel, lasagna pasta and lot of fun!

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna egg pasta 250g-300 g
  • Grated cheese 150 g

Ragu (Sauce):

  • 500 g minced meat
  • 1 onion
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery
  • 1 glass red wine
  • pepper and salt
  • 3-4 tablespoons oil extra vergin olive

Bechamel:

  • 1l milk
  • 100g butter
  • 100g flour
  • fine salt - to taste
  • nutmeg - to taste

Directions

  1. For the sauce: After you have cut the carrot, celery and onion into small pieces, brown them in a pan with 3-4 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil and the bacon, cut it into small pieces too.
  2. For the bechamel: Melt the butter in a saucepan, then sprinkle in the flour. Pour in the milk slowly, season with salt and add the nutmeg, then bring to the boil. Stir continuously until the bechamel thickens.
  3. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a little bechamel on the bottom of a pan and arrange the lasagne sheets on top. Cover with the meat sauce, a layer of bechamel and a spoonful of grated cheese. Repeat these steps several times, then place in the oven at 180 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
