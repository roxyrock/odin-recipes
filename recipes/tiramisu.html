A delicious tiramisu recipe for an unforgettable culinary experience

Coffee, mascarpone, cocoa powder, ladyfingers sponge cakes and lot of fun!

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 80 g sugar
  • 400g mascarpone
  • 4 cups of coffee
  • 2 spoons cacao powder

Steps:

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolk. The egg yolks must be whipped with the sugar until they become a cream.
  2. The egg whites must be whipped until stiff, and then the mascarpone is added with delicate movements so as not to flatten the egg white.
  3. Mix the mascarpone cream with the yolk cream with slow movements from bottom to top.
  4. Assembling the tiramisu: in a baking dish, place a layer of cream, a layer of ladyfingers soaked in coffee, then cream again, until we run out of ingredients. We cover the last layer of cream with cocoa.
  5. Put the tiramisu in the fridge. It is consumed cold and can be stored refrigerated for a maximum of 3 days.
